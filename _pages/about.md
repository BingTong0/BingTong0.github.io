I am a third-year PhD student in the joint program between [CreateLink](https://www.galaxybase.com/) and [HKUST(GZ)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/), under the supervision of [Prof. Jia Li](https://sites.google.com/view/lijia).

## Interests

My research interests mainly focus on graph data management and large language model (LLM) systems, particularly distributed graph databases, GraphRAG, and memory-augmented AI systems. Specifically, my research spans the following topics:

- **Graph × LLM**: GraphRAG for enterprise knowledge systems, graph-based memory for intelligent agents, and LLM-powered knowledge systems over structured enterprise data.
- **Distributed graph data management**: native distributed graph databases, graph transformation and optimization, and scalable systems for large-scale graph data processing.
- **Graph benchmarking and algorithms**: graph benchmarks, transactional workload evaluation, and distributed graph algorithms for performance and scalability analysis.
